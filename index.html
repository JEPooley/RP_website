About Me

Hi, my name is Robert Pooley, and I am a maths tutor working from my home in Devon, England.

I am now very pleased to be able to offer all GCSE and A-level maths students an exciting new ONLINE coaching service to supplement their school and sixth-form studies.

I have a wide range of work experience, mainly in software development, acoustics research, and as a craftsman potter, but with brief spells in government administration and farming. I have been tutoring maths and physics for more than ten years.

I have a masters in maths from Cambridge University, and I am a member of the Tutor's Association

One-to-one Tuition in the Comfort of Your Own Home

Online tutorials take place on a shared state-of-the-art virtual whiteboard with audio and video link-up to recreate all the benefits of traditional face-to-face coaching. There will be about 50 minutes online teaching within an hourly booking.

Just book an hourly slot for your convenience (see below) and meet me on the whiteboard at the time agreed.

Full help setting up for first time users makes getting started easy.

I offer a free, half-hour, no-strings introductory setting-up session when we can meet on a whiteboard and discuss your requirements in more detail.

The unique advantages of one-to-one tuition mean that the style and content of each and every tutorial can be tailored to your requirements. For instance, I find that many students want a full course of study, whilst others have particular topics which they want to pursue. Many, of course, want immediate help preparing for exams.

Internet Connection

There can, on occasion, be an internet connection problem which means that either a lesson cannot commence, or that a lesson is terminated part-way through.

If there is a connection problem which means that a lesson cannot commence, then this will not be charged.

If connection problems mean that a lesson has to be abandoned having got underway, then the lesson will be charged, but I will make up any lost time at a later time/date.

Missed or Cancelled Appointments

From time-to-time, students may have to cancel lessons, and I do not charge a cancellation fee.

Nevertheless, repeated or persistent cancellation is a charge on my time, as it needlessly denies others the use of the time slot. I would therefore seek to resolve a situation like this with the student and their parent/guardian as soon as a problem is identified.
