Fees

GCSE and A-Level

Fees are £25 per hour for KS3, GCSE and A-Level Maths and for all examination boards including AQA, Edexcel and OCR.

Initial no-strings one-hour taster session - £12.50.

Half hour no-strings consultation session - FREE.

Further Maths and University Entrance

Fees are £30 per hour for Further A-Level Maths, for university entrance work, and for help with first year university work.

Initial no-strings one-hour taster session - £15.

Half hour no-strings consultation session - FREE.

Means of Payment

Payment is usually per lesson, either by cash or by BACS.

Missed Lessons

Obviously, there can be reasons for a student cancelling a lesson from time to time, and I do not charge a cancellation fee for this.

Nevertheless, repeated or persistent cancellation is a charge on everyone's time, and this situation would need to be resolved with the student and parent/guardian as soon as a problem is identified.
