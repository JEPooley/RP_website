Fees

Cost of a Session

The charge for online lessons is £15 for one 45 minute session, and includes homework, if the tutee so requires.

This fee applies to each 45 minute session, regardless of subject, level, or syllabus.

Payment is by BACS.

Internet Connection Problems

If there are connection problems which mean that a lesson cannot take place, then this will not be charged. If the lesson is interrupted having got underway, then the lesson will be charged, but I will make up any lost time at a later time/date.

Missed or Cancelled Appointments

There can and will be reasons for a student either cancelling or missing a lesson from time to time, and I do not charge a cancellation fee.

Nevertheless, repeated or persistent cancellation is a charge on my time, and it needlessly denies others the use of the time slot. A situation like this would therefore need to be resolved with the student and parent/guardian as soon as a problem is identified.
